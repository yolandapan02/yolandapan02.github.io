--- title: "Beyond Borders: Pechoin's Global Expansion Strategy from Malaysia to Southeast Asia" date: 2025-02-22 draft: false ---

This project represents comprehensive strategic analysis for Pechoin, a leading Chinese skincare brand. As team leader for this Oliver Wyman Impact Competition entry, I developed a data-driven expansion strategy identifying Malaysia as the optimal entry point into Southeast Asian markets based on consumer preferences, competitive landscape, and cultural alignment.

The project addressed critical business challenges through quantitative analysis of beauty industry trends and consumer data. Our strategy integrated a three-pillar brand positioning, tailored product matrix, omni-channel approach, and comprehensive supply chain solution. Financial modeling projected a pathway to $20 million in annual profits by 2030, with planned expansion to Singapore and Indonesia in years 3-5, demonstrating how systematic market analysis creates actionable strategies in competitive markets.
